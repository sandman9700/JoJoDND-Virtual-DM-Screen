Key Rule Changes

- Arcana Checks will be used to help you determine unexplained phenomena. A successful Arcana Check will reveal if an item has been infused with a special energy, or if it is just a trick that can be explained through conventional technology. As a Reaction, when you are affected/damaged by an unknown phenomenon that you suspect is an ability, you may make an Arcana Check in order to determine the function of the ability.

- Initiative is calculated by adding your Dexterity AND Wisdom instead of just Dex. Stand stats can not be used to calculate initiative.

- Talking is a free action.

- The Tile System is meter-based. Each 'square' represents 1 meter.

- Bonus Proficiencies are granted based on a character's Intelligence Modifier. You may pick that number of Skills (minimum of zero) and gain Proficiency in them.

- Armor Class (AC) is calculated as either 10 + Dex + Con, 10 + Dex + Wis, or 10 + Wis + Con. The same calculation applies to Stands, with their respective stats.

- Medicine Checks may be performed on a character with 0 Hit Points. If you succeed the Medicine Check (DC 10), they return to 1 Hit Point.

- Inspiration has no upper limit in terms of how much you can have at once, and it persists over a short or long rest.

- Unarmed Strikes deal 1d4 Bludgeoning Damage. You may choose to add either your Strength OR Dexterity to the Attack Rolls, but only Strength may be added to the Damage Rolls.

Weapon Reference

Weapon Attributes

  • Ambush: Whenever you attack a target who is Surprised with this weapon, if you hit your target, you automatically deal a critical hit.
  • Bulky: Before you use this weapon to attack, you must use your Bonus Action to Brace.
  • Complex: Rather than taking one attack to reload, this weapon requires three attacks to reload.
  • Concealed: Gives a bonus equal to the number provided by this attribute on all attempts made to conceal the weapon.
  • Firearm: Creates a loud noise when fired, ammo does not function when wet, and when a nat 1 is rolled on attack rolls with a weapon with this attribute, the weapon becomes jammed, and an Action must be spent to make it function again.
  • Flame: Whenever the target of the attack takes fire damage, if the target is flammable, they are set ablaze.
  • Light: When you wield two weapons with the Light property at once, you may make an additional attack without penalty on your turn as a Bonus Action using either weapon.
  • Point Blank: You may make ranged attacks in melee without penalty. Additionally, when you are within melee range of a prone target, you have advantage to hit them with attacks made with this weapon.
  • Radius: The range in which you must make a Dexterity Saving Throw against an explosive.
  • Ranged: This weapon can fire up to its short range (the top number), or as far as its long range (the bottom number) with Disadvantage. Firing within melee range imposes Disadvantage.
  • Reliable: A firearm that does not jam when a nat 1 is rolled on an attack roll.
  • Reload: How many times the weapon must be fired before an attack must be spent to reload.
  • Remote: An explosive that may be detonated as a Bonus Action, up to 1 kilometer away.
  • Return: A er making a Thrown Weapon attack with the weapon, you may use your Reaction to catch it, putting it back in your inventory.
  • Siege: Deals double damage against objects.
  • Timer: You may set an amount of time for an explosive device. A er the timer runs out, the explosive detonates. Can be set for up to 12 hours between priming and detonation.
  • Thrown: This weapon can be thrown to its short range (the top number), or as far as its long range (the bottom number) with Disadvantage. Throwing within melee range imposes Disadvantage.
  • Two-Handed: This weapon required two hands to attack with. May be carried using a singular hand.

Demolitionist

  • Proficiency in Explosives & Expertise in them if you already are proficient with them.
  • Explosive Save DC +2
  • Advantage on anything explosive related

Heavy Specialization

  • Proficiency in Heavy Weapons & Expertise in them if you already are proficient with them.
  • +2 to your AC until start of next turn when bracing with a heavy weapon

Martial Arts

  • Proficiency in Improvised Weapons & Expertise in them if you already are proficient with them.
  • Expertise in unarmed strikes and stand melee
  • 1d8 unarmed strikes
  • When Grappling or Shoving, you can use Dex or Str
  • Full action Grapple, If you are successful, the target is restrained indefinitely until you use your action for something else or you move.

Melee Specialization

  • Proficiency in Melee Weapons & Expertise in them if you already are proficient with them.
  • 1/turn you may reroll damage dice for a melee weapon
  • While wielding yo can take the 5e Parry (Add PB to AC) reaction if hit

Professional Weapon Training

  • Proficiency in Simple and Martial Weapons & Expertise in them if you already are proficient with them.

Rifle Specialization

  • Proficiency in Improvised Weapons & Expertise in them if you already are proficient with them.
  • A 19 or 20 is a crit with Rifles
  • No disadvantage at long range with Rifle

Shotgun Specialization

  • Proficiency in Shotguns & Expertise in them if you already are proficient with them.
  • When shooting medium or smaller creature at short range, you can choose to halve damage to force them to make a DC 8 + Con Mod +PB Con save, On a fail they are knocked prone

Sidearm Specialization

  • Proficiency in Sidearms & Expertise in them if you already are proficient with them.
  • Free action reload with Sidearms

Simple Weapon Focus

  • Proficiency in Simple Weapons & Expertise in them if you already are proficient with them.
  • Add PB to damage on Melee, or a second time to attack roll with ranged

Tavern Brawler

  • Proficiency in Improvised Weapons & Expertise in them if you already are proficient with them.
  • When attacking on your turn, you may make an additional improvised weapon attack as a bonus action, or Grapple/Shove a target as a bonus action

Thrown Weapon Specialization

  • Proficiency in Thrown Weapons & Expertise in them if you already are proficient with them.
  • Draw a Thrown weapon as a free action
  • Throw 2 same thrown/improvised weapons as a single attack. This cannot be done with spin spheres
